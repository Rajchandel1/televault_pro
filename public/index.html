About TeleVault Pro

TeleVault Pro is the best free alternative to Google Drive, Dropbox, and iCloud for unlimited photo and video storage. Built on Telegram's secure infrastructure, your data stays private and accessible.

Why Choose TeleVault?

  • 100% Free Forever: No hidden charges, no subscriptions, no premium plans.
  • Unlimited Storage: Store as many photos and videos as you want.
  • Maximum Privacy: Files stored in your own private Telegram channel.
  • Cross-Platform: Works on all devices via web browser or PWA install.
  • No Compression: Original quality preserved.
  • Fast & Reliable: Powered by Telegram's global infrastructure.

How TeleVault Works

  1. Create your account with just a name and password
  2. Connect via Quick Connect (bot chat) or Private Channel
  3. Upload photos and videos instantly
  4. Organize with folders and access from anywhere

Compare TeleVault with Alternatives

Unlike Google Photos (15GB limit), Dropbox (2GB free), or iCloud (5GB free), TeleVault offers truly unlimited storage at zero cost. Perfect for photographers, content creators, and anyone tired of cloud storage limits.
