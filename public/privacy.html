Privacy Policy

Last updated: june 2026

TeleVault Pro respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We keep it short and honest.

1. The Short Version

  • We don't store your files on our servers — they live in your private Telegram channel
  • We don't sell your data to anyone, ever
  • We don't show ads or track you across the web
  • We don't read your personal Telegram chats
  • We store minimal data needed to make the service work

2. What Data We Collect

Account Information

  • Your Telegram first name (e.g., "Raj")
  • Your phone number (used only for OTP login)
  • A randomly generated user ID
  • Account creation timestamp

Session Data (For Each Device You Log In On)

  • Encrypted session token (keeps you logged in, like WhatsApp Web)
  • Device information (browser type, OS — e.g., "Chrome on Windows")
  • Device fingerprint (anonymous hash to recognize your device)
  • Last activity timestamp

File Metadata (References Only)

  • File names you upload
  • File sizes and types (image/video)
  • Telegram message IDs (to retrieve files later)
  • Folder organization data

What We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ Your actual files (they're in your Telegram channel, not our servers)
  • ❌ Your Telegram password or 2FA codes
  • ❌ Your personal Telegram chats with friends/family
  • ❌ Your contacts
  • ❌ Analytics, tracking pixels, or cookies for advertising
  • ❌ IP addresses (beyond standard server logs)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Authentication: Phone number and session tokens keep you logged in
  • File access: Metadata helps display your files in the gallery
  • Service operation: That's it. We don't analyze, sell, or share your data

4. Where Your Files Live

Your files are stored in YOUR own private Telegram channel, not on TeleVault's servers. TeleVault acts as a beautiful interface to upload and view those files.

This means:

  • You own and control your files completely
  • If TeleVault disappears tomorrow, your files remain in your Telegram
  • You can remove TeleVault's access anytime by deleting the channel or logging out

5. How We Protect Your Data

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Telegram's MTProto encryption for file transfers (industry standard)
  • Secure database with restricted access (Supabase)
  • Per-device sessions — losing one device doesn't compromise others
  • No password storage for OTP users (random placeholder used)

6. Third-Party Services We Use

Telegram

TeleVault uses Telegram's MTProto API to store your files. Telegram has its own Privacy Policy. You authenticate via Telegram's official OTP system.

Supabase (Database)

We use Supabase to store account metadata. They're SOC 2 compliant and GDPR-ready. Supabase Privacy Policy.

Render (Hosting)

Our servers run on Render. They handle standard server logs. Render Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: Everything we store is visible in your Settings
  • Delete your account: Contact us and we'll remove your data
  • Logout all devices: Available in Settings (revokes all sessions)
  • Take your files: They're in your Telegram channel — you already have them
  • Stop using TeleVault: Anytime, no questions asked

8. Children's Privacy

TeleVault is not intended for users under 13 years old. We don't knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data kept while account is in use
  • Inactive accounts (6+ months): Sessions auto-expire
  • Deleted accounts: All data removed within 30 days

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated via the app. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

12. The Bottom Line

TeleVault is built by an independent developer who believes in user privacy. We don't have investors pressuring us to monetize your data. We don't run ads. We don't sell anything.

Your files are yours. Your data is yours. We're just the bridge that makes it look beautiful and work seamlessly.

If you ever feel uncomfortable, you can leave — and take everything with you (because it was always yours).
